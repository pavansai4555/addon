TalentServe Resume Metrics and Comparision AI tool

TalentServe resume metrics and comparison Al is built based on how companies and their software robots analyze your resume after parsing knowledge and data points from it.

7 metrics are (and we will keep on adding more metrics we research with them)

1. Cultural Fit - What is your resume's cultural fit for a company.? (4 metrics that add up to 100 Percent-philosophical, practical, personal ,social)

2. Personality type- What is your personality type for them ? MBTI personality type is most commonly used majorly by everyone. There are 16 personality types identified from text that add up to 100 percent. It is possible that your resume text has more than 3 personality types out of 16. It is also possible that job description text or/and hiring person's recent post on Linkedln or/and technical manager's Linkedln profiles text/posts text also have those same 3 personality types. You just have to compare ,asses , edit, personalize before applying.

3. Readability Metrics - Which target age group can read, understand and comprehend your resume? If your resume text's readability 'text standard' metric says 22nd grade and above can understand it., that means 12th std guys age (which is 18) + 10 yrs (making the grade 22nd) but his/her age 28 and above can understand and comprehend your resume text. You should compare edit and personalize before applying.

4. Difficult words- Difficult words are of syllable greater than 2. If Job description text or/and hiring person's recent Linkedln post text has less difficult words and your resume text has more, there is a 80 percent chance of mismatch. Compare, edit and bring them equal to theirs before personalized customized applying.

5. Discourse - Discourse has 5 metrics. How much percentage a person agrees, anticipates, elaborates, disagrees, appreciates, negative about something in his/her job description or/and hiring post text versus your resume text for same metrics- they should match before personalized, customized applying.

6. News content comparison - How much a job description text has content related to latest 1 hour news, versus your resume.

7. Depression 6 causes- Avoid text in your resume that has high percentages of irritability, hopelessness, anxiousness, guilt, low self esteem, sadness. This might make the Hiring person depressed or not positive about your resume text. Don't try to evaluate these percentages in job description text, hiring person's posts or Linkedln profiles. It wont work.
